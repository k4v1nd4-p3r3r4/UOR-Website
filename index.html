Degree We Offer

The Faculty of Technology was officially established on 2016 with four Departments, namely,
Engineering Technology, Information and Communication Technology, Biosystems Technology and Multidisciplinary Studies.

Bachelor of Engineering Technology Honours - BET (Hons)

TEngineering Technology Honours degree program offers four-year, full-time studies in various fields, mandatory industrial training, and multidisciplinary studies.

Information and Communication Technology Honours - BICT (Hons)

The Department of Information and Communication Technology offers a Bachelor of Information and Communication Technology Honours degree in Application Development, preparing students for professional careers in ICT.

Degree of Biosystems Technology Honours - BBST (Hons)

Department of Biosystems Technology offers Honours Degree Program, focusing on Agricultural, Biomedical, Bioprocessing, Environmental Biosystems, encompassing Basic Science, Statistics, ICT, Entrepreneurship, and Management.
